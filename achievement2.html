About Abhigyan'21 and Experience

Abhigyan'21 is a National level symposium which is organized by SRM Institute of Science and Technology by the depatment of Electronicas and Communication Engineering. It was my first Symposium I had appeared and secured winner award for my paper presentation. It had been the place to showcase my talent and knowledge.

Abstract

The SARS-CoV-2 epidemic has divided disaster risk management around the world. The Blockchain embedded in the healthcare sector could provide new and effective opportunities to improve many of the services related to trauma and disease management and, therefore, high-risk clinical disaster management within the context of epidemics such as this. Blockchain has been widely used in attention management, as a strategic tool to strengthen collaborative agreements that go hand in hand with building the right foundation for a supportive and effective decision-making process. We have a tendency to aim to ensure blockchain attention and recommend the traceroute to follow the safe clinics of COVID19. With this paper, we have a habit of reviewing various programs and traceroute of information provided to patient and medical care in hospitals and the public sector via blockchain. Blockchain deployment and AI programs allow for the development of a standardized system that will contribute to the detection of epidemic risk in the national area. SWOT analysis of blockchain-based predictive model acceptance with care and infection of SARS-CoV-2 is given to underline the possibilities and limitations of its adoption. Blockchain could play a strategic role in future digital healthcare: Specifically, it will work to improve COVID19 compliance. Many theories, and especially those associated with clinical progress, found in blockchain-based models are rumored here and have been criticized.

Keywords:COVID-19, Healthcare management, Global health,Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence
