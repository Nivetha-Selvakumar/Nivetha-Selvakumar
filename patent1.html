  • Status: Published

About Patent

A patent is an exclusive right granted for an invention, which is a product or a process that provides, in general, a new way of doing something, or offers a new technical solution to a problem. I had filed a patent of my idea in healthcare management using Blockchain technology.

Field of Invention

The catastrophe risk management field typically refers to the present invention. The healthcare industry's integration of blockchain technology may offer fresh and efficient ways to enhance a variety of services related to the management of trauma and disease, and as a result, high-risk clinical disaster management in the setting of epidemics like this one. In order to improve collaborative agreements that go hand in hand with laying the proper groundwork for a helpful and efficient decision-making process, blockchain has been frequently employed in attention management.

Claims

  • Improved Security: A digital healthcare management system using block-chain can provide a highly secure platform for storing and sharing sensitive medical data, as the technology utilizes advanced cryptography and distributed ledger technology to ensure data integrity and privacy.
  • Efficient Record Management: Block-chain-based healthcare management systems can significantly streamline the record management process by allowing patients, doctors, and other healthcare providers to access medical records in real-time, reducing the need for redundant data entry and manual record keeping.
  • Better Interoperability: By leveraging blockchain technology, digital healthcare management systems can help improve interoperability between different healthcare providers and systems, facilitating the seamless exchange of medical data and enabling better coordinated care.
